Twin Sun Smoothie

Description

Refreshing fruit smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup frozen peaches
  • 1 small banana
  • Juice of half of a pink lemon

Steps

  1. In a blender add the orange juice, strawberries, peaches, banana and the lemon juice. Blend until combined then pour into a tall glass.
  2. Garnish with pink lemon slices.
