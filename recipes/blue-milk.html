Blue Milk

Description

A staple drink loved by farmers and Jedi alike!

Very simple two step recipe

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla
  • Blue food gel dye

Steps

  1. Stir together the milk, sugar, and vanilla.
  2. When the sugar is dissolved, stir in a drop of blue food gel dye until combined.
